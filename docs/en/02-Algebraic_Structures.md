# Algebraic Structures (代数结构)

This document provides bilingual (English-Chinese) definitions, notations, and examples for 25 core concepts in algebraic structures.

---

## 1. Binary Operation (二元运算)

### English Definition
A binary operation on a set $S$ is a function $\ast: S \times S \to S$ that assigns to each ordered pair $(a, b) \in S \times S$ a unique element $a \ast b \in S$.

### 中文定义
集合$S$上的二元运算是一个函数$\ast: S \times S \to S$，它将每个有序对$(a, b) \in S \times S$映射到唯一的元素$a \ast b \in S$。

### Notation
- $a \ast b$: result of operation $\ast$ on $a$ and $b$
- Common operations: $+, \cdot, \times, \circ$
- $(S, \ast)$: set with operation

### Example
- Addition on $\mathbb{Z}$: $(a, b) \mapsto a + b$
- Composition of functions: $(f, g) \mapsto f \circ g$

---

## 2. Group (群)

### English Definition
A group $(G, \cdot)$ is a set $G$ with a binary operation $\cdot$ satisfying: (1) Associativity: $(a \cdot b) \cdot c = a \cdot (b \cdot c)$; (2) Identity: $\exists e \in G$ such that $e \cdot a = a \cdot e = a$; (3) Inverses: $\forall a \in G, \exists a^{-1} \in G$ such that $a \cdot a^{-1} = a^{-1} \cdot a = e$.

### 中文定义
群$(G, \cdot)$是一个集合$G$配上一个二元运算$\cdot$，满足：(1)结合律：$(a \cdot b) \cdot c = a \cdot (b \cdot c)$；(2)单位元：存在$e \in G$使得$e \cdot a = a \cdot e = a$；(3)逆元：对每个$a \in G$，存在$a^{-1} \in G$使得$a \cdot a^{-1} = a^{-1} \cdot a = e$。

### Notation
- $(G, \cdot)$ or simply $G$: group
- $e$ or $e_G$: identity element
- $a^{-1}$: inverse of $a$
- $|G|$: order of the group

### Example
- $(\mathbb{Z}, +)$: integers under addition, identity $0$
- $(\mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}, \times)$: non-zero reals under multiplication
- Symmetric group $S_n$: permutations of $\{1, 2, \ldots, n\}$

---

## 3. Abelian Group (阿贝尔群)

### English Definition
An abelian group is a group $(G, \cdot)$ in which the operation is commutative: $a \cdot b = b \cdot a$ for all $a, b \in G$.

### 中文定义
阿贝尔群是运算满足交换律的群$(G, \cdot)$：对所有$a, b \in G$有$a \cdot b = b \cdot a$。

### Notation
- Abelian groups often written with additive notation $(G, +)$
- Identity denoted $0$, inverse denoted $-a$

### Example
- $(\mathbb{Z}, +), (\mathbb{Q}, +), (\mathbb{R}, +)$ are abelian groups
- $(\mathbb{R}^n, +)$: vector addition
- Any cyclic group is abelian

---

## 4. Subgroup (子群)

### English Definition
A subgroup $H$ of a group $G$, denoted $H \leq G$, is a subset of $G$ that is itself a group under the same operation. Equivalently, $H \neq \emptyset$ and $H$ is closed under the operation and inverses.

### 中文定义
群$G$的子群$H$，记作$H \leq G$，是$G$的一个子集，它在相同的运算下本身构成一个群。等价地，$H \neq \emptyset$且$H$对运算和取逆元封闭。

### Notation
- $H \leq G$: $H$ is a subgroup of $G$
- $H < G$: proper subgroup ($H \neq G$)
- $\langle S \rangle$: subgroup generated by set $S$

### Example
- $2\mathbb{Z} = \{2n : n \in \mathbb{Z}\}$ is a subgroup of $(\mathbb{Z}, +)$
- $A_n$ (alternating group) is a subgroup of $S_n$

---

## 5. Cyclic Group (循环群)

### English Definition
A cyclic group is a group generated by a single element: $G = \langle g \rangle = \{g^n : n \in \mathbb{Z}\}$. Cyclic groups are always abelian.

### 中文定义
循环群是由单个元素生成的群：$G = \langle g \rangle = \{g^n : n \in \mathbb{Z}\}$。循环群总是阿贝尔群。

### Notation
- $\langle g \rangle$: cyclic subgroup generated by $g$
- $|g|$ or $\text{ord}(g)$: order of element $g$
- $C_n$ or $\mathbb{Z}_n$: cyclic group of order $n$

### Example
- $(\mathbb{Z}, +) = \langle 1 \rangle = \langle -1 \rangle$ is infinite cyclic
- $\mathbb{Z}_n = \{0, 1, \ldots, n-1\}$ under addition modulo $n$

---

## 6. Normal Subgroup (正规子群)

### English Definition
A subgroup $N$ of $G$ is normal, denoted $N \trianglelefteq G$, if $gNg^{-1} = N$ for all $g \in G$, or equivalently, $gN = Ng$ for all $g \in G$.

### 中文定义
群$G$的子群$N$是正规子群，记作$N \trianglelefteq G$，如果对所有$g \in G$有$gNg^{-1} = N$，或等价地，对所有$g \in G$有$gN = Ng$。

### Notation
- $N \trianglelefteq G$: normal subgroup
- $N \triangleleft G$: proper normal subgroup
- $gNg^{-1} = \{gng^{-1} : n \in N\}$: conjugate of $N$

### Example
- Every subgroup of an abelian group is normal
- $A_n \trianglelefteq S_n$ for $n \geq 2$

---

## 7. Quotient Group (商群)

### English Definition
Given a normal subgroup $N \trianglelefteq G$, the quotient group $G/N$ is the set of cosets $\{gN : g \in G\}$ with operation $(gN)(hN) = (gh)N$.

### 中文定义
给定正规子群$N \trianglelefteq G$，商群$G/N$是陪集的集合$\{gN : g \in G\}$，运算为$(gN)(hN) = (gh)N$。

### Notation
- $G/N$: quotient group
- $gN = \{gn : n \in N\}$: left coset of $N$
- $|G/N| = |G|/|N|$ (Lagrange's theorem for finite groups)

### Example
- $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} \cong \mathbb{Z}_n$
- $S_n/A_n$ has order 2

---

## 8. Group Homomorphism (群同态)

### English Definition
A group homomorphism $\varphi: G \to H$ is a function between groups preserving the operation: $\varphi(g_1 \cdot g_2) = \varphi(g_1) \cdot \varphi(g_2)$.

### 中文定义
群同态$\varphi: G \to H$是群之间的函数，保持运算：$\varphi(g_1 \cdot g_2) = \varphi(g_1) \cdot \varphi(g_2)$。

### Notation
- $\varphi: G \to H$: homomorphism
- $\ker(\varphi) = \{g \in G : \varphi(g) = e_H\}$: kernel
- $\text{Im}(\varphi) = \{\varphi(g) : g \in G\}$: image

### Example
- $\varphi: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_n$, $\varphi(k) = k \pmod{n}$
- det: $GL_n(\mathbb{R}) \to \mathbb{R}^\times$, determinant map

---

## 9. Group Isomorphism (群同构)

### English Definition
A group isomorphism is a bijective homomorphism. Two groups are isomorphic ($G \cong H$) if there exists an isomorphism between them, meaning they have the same algebraic structure.

### 中文定义
群同构是双射的同态。如果两个群之间存在同构，则称它们同构（$G \cong H$），意味着它们具有相同的代数结构。

### Notation
- $\varphi: G \xrightarrow{\sim} H$: isomorphism
- $G \cong H$: $G$ is isomorphic to $H$
- $\text{Aut}(G)$: group of automorphisms of $G$

### Example
- $\mathbb{Z}_6 \cong \mathbb{Z}_2 \times \mathbb{Z}_3$
- $\mathbb{R}^+ \cong \mathbb{R}$ via logarithm map

---

## 10. First Isomorphism Theorem (第一同构定理)

### English Definition
If $\varphi: G \to H$ is a group homomorphism, then $G/\ker(\varphi) \cong \text{Im}(\varphi)$. This fundamental theorem connects homomorphisms, kernels, and quotient groups.

### 中文定义
如果$\varphi: G \to H$是群同态，则$G/\ker(\varphi) \cong \text{Im}(\varphi)$。这个基本定理将同态、核与商群联系起来。

### Notation
- $\ker(\varphi)$: kernel of homomorphism
- $G/\ker(\varphi)$: quotient by kernel
- $\bar{\varphi}: G/\ker(\varphi) \to \text{Im}(\varphi)$: induced isomorphism

### Example
- $\varphi: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_n$, $\ker(\varphi) = n\mathbb{Z}$, so $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} \cong \mathbb{Z}_n$

## 11. Ring (环)

### English Definition
A ring $(R, +, \cdot)$ is a set with two operations: $(R, +)$ is an abelian group, multiplication is associative, and distributive laws hold: $a(b+c) = ab + ac$ and $(b+c)a = ba + ca$.

### 中文定义
环$(R, +, \cdot)$是一个具有两种运算的集合：$(R, +)$是阿贝尔群，乘法满足结合律，且满足分配律：$a(b+c) = ab + ac$和$(b+c)a = ba + ca$。

### Notation
- $0$: additive identity
- $1$ or $1_R$: multiplicative identity (if exists)
- $-a$: additive inverse
- $R^\times$: group of units (invertible elements)

### Example
- $(\mathbb{Z}, +, \cdot)$: integers
- $M_n(\mathbb{R})$: $n \times n$ matrices
- $\mathbb{Z}[x]$: polynomials with integer coefficients

---

## 12. Commutative Ring (交换环)

### English Definition
A commutative ring is a ring in which multiplication is commutative: $ab = ba$ for all $a, b \in R$.

### 中文定义
交换环是乘法满足交换律的环：对所有$a, b \in R$有$ab = ba$。

### Notation
- $ab = ba$: commutativity
- $\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{R}, \mathbb{C}$ are commutative rings

### Example
- $\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{R}, \mathbb{C}$ with standard operations
- $\mathbb{Z}[x]$: polynomial ring
- $\mathbb{Z}_n$: integers modulo $n$

---

## 13. Field (域)

### English Definition
A field is a commutative ring with identity $1 \neq 0$ in which every non-zero element has a multiplicative inverse.

### 中文定义
域是一个具有单位元$1 \neq 0$的交换环，其中每个非零元素都有乘法逆元。

### Notation
- $F$ or $K$: field
- $a^{-1}$ or $1/a$: multiplicative inverse
- $\text{char}(F)$: characteristic of field

### Example
- $\mathbb{Q}, \mathbb{R}, \mathbb{C}$ are fields
- $\mathbb{Z}_p$ is a field when $p$ is prime
- $\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt{2}) = \{a + b\sqrt{2} : a, b \in \mathbb{Q}\}$

---

## 14. Ideal (理想)

### English Definition
An ideal $I$ of a ring $R$ is an additive subgroup such that $ri \in I$ and $ir \in I$ for all $r \in R, i \in I$ (two-sided ideal).

### 中文定义
环$R$的理想$I$是一个加法子群，满足对所有$r \in R, i \in I$有$ri \in I$和$ir \in I$（双边理想）。

### Notation
- $I \trianglelefteq R$: ideal
- $(a) = aR = \{ar : r \in R\}$: principal ideal generated by $a$
- $I + J$, $IJ$: sum and product of ideals

### Example
- $n\mathbb{Z}$ is an ideal of $\mathbb{Z}$
- $(x)$ in $\mathbb{R}[x]$: polynomials with zero constant term

---

## 15. Quotient Ring (商环)

### English Definition
Given an ideal $I$ of ring $R$, the quotient ring $R/I$ consists of cosets $\{r + I : r \in R\}$ with operations $(r+I) + (s+I) = (r+s) + I$ and $(r+I)(s+I) = rs + I$.

### 中文定义
给定环$R$的理想$I$，商环$R/I$由陪集$\{r + I : r \in R\}$组成，运算为$(r+I) + (s+I) = (r+s) + I$和$(r+I)(s+I) = rs + I$。

### Notation
- $R/I$: quotient ring
- $r + I$: coset of $I$
- $\pi: R \to R/I$: canonical projection

### Example
- $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} \cong \mathbb{Z}_n$
- $\mathbb{R}[x]/(x^2 + 1) \cong \mathbb{C}$

---

## 16. Ring Homomorphism (环同态)

### English Definition
A ring homomorphism $\varphi: R \to S$ preserves both operations: $\varphi(a + b) = \varphi(a) + \varphi(b)$ and $\varphi(ab) = \varphi(a)\varphi(b)$. If rings have identities, often require $\varphi(1_R) = 1_S$.

### 中文定义
环同态$\varphi: R \to S$保持两种运算：$\varphi(a + b) = \varphi(a) + \varphi(b)$和$\varphi(ab) = \varphi(a)\varphi(b)$。如果环有单位元，通常要求$\varphi(1_R) = 1_S$。

### Notation
- $\varphi: R \to S$: ring homomorphism
- $\ker(\varphi) = \{r : \varphi(r) = 0\}$: kernel (always an ideal)

### Example
- Modulo map: $\mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{Z}_n$
- Evaluation map: $\mathbb{R}[x] \to \mathbb{R}$, $p(x) \mapsto p(a)$

---

## 17. Vector Space (向量空间)

### English Definition
A vector space $V$ over a field $F$ is an abelian group $(V, +)$ with scalar multiplication $F \times V \to V$ satisfying: $a(v + w) = av + aw$, $(a + b)v = av + bv$, $a(bv) = (ab)v$, and $1v = v$.

### 中文定义
域$F$上的向量空间$V$是一个阿贝尔群$(V, +)$配上数乘$F \times V \to V$，满足：$a(v + w) = av + aw$，$(a + b)v = av + bv$，$a(bv) = (ab)v$，以及$1v = v$。

### Notation
- $V$ or $W$: vector space
- $\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w}$: vectors
- $a, b, c$ or $\alpha, \beta$: scalars
- $\mathbf{0}$: zero vector

### Example
- $\mathbb{R}^n$: $n$-dimensional Euclidean space
- $F[x]$: polynomials over field $F$
- $C([0,1])$: continuous functions on $[0,1]$

---

## 18. Subspace (子空间)

### English Definition
A subspace $W$ of a vector space $V$ is a subset that is itself a vector space under the same operations. Equivalently, $W \neq \emptyset$ and is closed under addition and scalar multiplication.

### 中文定义
向量空间$V$的子空间$W$是在相同运算下本身构成向量空间的子集。等价地，$W \neq \emptyset$且对加法和数乘封闭。

### Notation
- $W \leq V$: $W$ is a subspace of $V$
- $\text{span}(S)$: subspace generated by set $S$

### Example
- Lines through origin in $\mathbb{R}^2$ are subspaces
- Solution space of homogeneous linear system $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$

---

## 19. Linear Combination (线性组合)

### English Definition
A linear combination of vectors $\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n$ is an expression $a_1\mathbf{v}_1 + a_2\mathbf{v}_2 + \cdots + a_n\mathbf{v}_n$ where $a_i \in F$ are scalars.

### 中文定义
向量$\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n$的线性组合是表达式$a_1\mathbf{v}_1 + a_2\mathbf{v}_2 + \cdots + a_n\mathbf{v}_n$，其中$a_i \in F$是标量。

### Notation
- $\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i\mathbf{v}_i$: linear combination
- $\text{span}\{\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n\}$: set of all linear combinations

### Example
- In $\mathbb{R}^2$, $3(1, 0) + 2(0, 1) = (3, 2)$
- Every vector in $\mathbb{R}^n$ is a linear combination of standard basis vectors

---

## 20. Linear Independence (线性无关)

### English Definition
Vectors $\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n$ are linearly independent if $a_1\mathbf{v}_1 + \cdots + a_n\mathbf{v}_n = \mathbf{0}$ implies $a_1 = a_2 = \cdots = a_n = 0$. Otherwise, they are linearly dependent.

### 中文定义
向量$\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n$线性无关，如果$a_1\mathbf{v}_1 + \cdots + a_n\mathbf{v}_n = \mathbf{0}$蕴含$a_1 = a_2 = \cdots = a_n = 0$。否则称它们线性相关。

### Notation
- Linearly independent: $\{\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n\}$ is LI set
- Linearly dependent: $\{\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n\}$ is LD set

### Example
- $\{(1, 0), (0, 1)\}$ in $\mathbb{R}^2$ is linearly independent
- $\{(1, 2), (2, 4)\}$ is linearly dependent (second is $2 \times$ first)

---

## 21. Basis (基)

### English Definition
A basis of a vector space $V$ is a linearly independent set that spans $V$. Every vector in $V$ can be written uniquely as a linear combination of basis vectors.

### 中文定义
向量空间$V$的基是一个线性无关的生成集。$V$中每个向量都可以唯一地表示为基向量的线性组合。

### Notation
- $\{\mathbf{e}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{e}_n\}$ or $\{\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n\}$: basis
- $\dim(V)$: dimension of $V$ (cardinality of basis)

### Example
- Standard basis of $\mathbb{R}^n$: $\{\mathbf{e}_1, \ldots, \mathbf{e}_n\}$ where $\mathbf{e}_i$ has 1 in $i$-th position
- $\{1, x, x^2, \ldots, x^n\}$ is a basis for polynomials of degree $\leq n$

---

## 22. Dimension (维数)

### English Definition
The dimension of a vector space $V$, denoted $\dim(V)$, is the number of vectors in any basis. All bases of a vector space have the same cardinality.

### 中文定义
向量空间$V$的维数，记作$\dim(V)$，是任意基中向量的个数。一个向量空间的所有基具有相同的基数。

### Notation
- $\dim(V)$ or $\dim_F(V)$: dimension
- $\dim(\mathbb{R}^n) = n$
- Infinite-dimensional: no finite basis exists

### Example
- $\dim(\mathbb{R}^n) = n$
- $\dim(F[x]) = \infty$ (infinite-dimensional)
- $\dim(M_{m \times n}(F)) = mn$

---

## 23. Linear Transformation (线性变换)

### English Definition
A linear transformation (or linear map) $T: V \to W$ between vector spaces satisfies: $T(\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v}) = T(\mathbf{u}) + T(\mathbf{v})$ and $T(a\mathbf{v}) = aT(\mathbf{v})$.

### 中文定义
向量空间之间的线性变换（或线性映射）$T: V \to W$满足：$T(\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v}) = T(\mathbf{u}) + T(\mathbf{v})$和$T(a\mathbf{v}) = aT(\mathbf{v})$。

### Notation
- $T, L, A$: linear transformations
- $\mathcal{L}(V, W)$: space of linear maps from $V$ to $W$
- $\text{End}(V) = \mathcal{L}(V, V)$: endomorphisms

### Example
- Matrix multiplication: $T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x}$
- Differentiation: $D: P_n \to P_{n-1}$, $D(p) = p'$
- Integration: $T(f) = \int_0^x f(t) dt$

---

## 24. Matrix Representation (矩阵表示)

### English Definition
Given a linear transformation $T: V \to W$ with bases $\mathcal{B} = \{\mathbf{v}_j\}$ of $V$ and $\mathcal{C} = \{\mathbf{w}_i\}$ of $W$, the matrix representation $[T]_{\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{B}}$ has entries $a_{ij}$ where $T(\mathbf{v}_j) = \sum_i a_{ij}\mathbf{w}_i$.

### 中文定义
给定线性变换$T: V \to W$，取$V$的基$\mathcal{B} = \{\mathbf{v}_j\}$和$W$的基$\mathcal{C} = \{\mathbf{w}_i\}$，矩阵表示$[T]_{\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{B}}$的元素$a_{ij}$满足$T(\mathbf{v}_j) = \sum_i a_{ij}\mathbf{w}_i$。

### Notation
- $[T]$ or $[T]_{\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{B}}$: matrix representation
- $A = (a_{ij})$: matrix with entry $a_{ij}$ in row $i$, column $j$

### Example
- Rotation by $\theta$ in $\mathbb{R}^2$: $\begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{pmatrix}$
- Projection onto $x$-axis: $\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$

---

## 25. Eigenvalue and Eigenvector (特征值与特征向量)

### English Definition
For a linear transformation $T: V \to V$, a non-zero vector $\mathbf{v}$ is an eigenvector with eigenvalue $\lambda$ if $T(\mathbf{v}) = \lambda\mathbf{v}$.

### 中文定义
对于线性变换$T: V \to V$，非零向量$\mathbf{v}$是特征值为$\lambda$的特征向量，如果$T(\mathbf{v}) = \lambda\mathbf{v}$。

### Notation
- $\lambda$: eigenvalue
- $\mathbf{v}$: eigenvector
- $E_\lambda = \{\mathbf{v} : T(\mathbf{v}) = \lambda\mathbf{v}\}$: eigenspace
- Characteristic polynomial: $\det(A - \lambda I) = 0$

### Example
- For $A = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{pmatrix}$, eigenvalues are $\lambda = 3, 1$
- Eigenvectors: $(1, 1)$ for $\lambda = 3$, $(1, -1)$ for $\lambda = 1$
