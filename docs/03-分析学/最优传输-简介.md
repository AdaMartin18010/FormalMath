# 最优传输简介

## Monge问题

### 表述
$$
\inf_{T: T_\#\mu = \nu} \int_X c(x, T(x)) d\mu(x)
$$

## Kantorovich松弛

### 表述
$$
\inf_{\gamma \in \Pi(\mu, \nu)} \int_{X \times Y} c(x,y) d\gamma(x,y)
$$

### Wasserstein距离
$$W_p(\mu, \nu) = \left(\inf_{\gamma} \int d(x,y)^p d\gamma\right)^{1/p}$$
