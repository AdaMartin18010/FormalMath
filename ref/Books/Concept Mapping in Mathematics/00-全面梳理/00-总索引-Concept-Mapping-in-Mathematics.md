## 📋 一、概述 / Overview

### 1.1 书籍信息 / Book Information

**书名 / Title**:

- 中文：数学中的概念映射：从研究到实践
- English: Concept Mapping in Mathematics: Research into Practice

**编辑 / Editor**:

- Karoline Afamasaga-Fuata'i
- University of New England, Australia

**出版信息 / Publication**:

- 出版社 / Publisher: Springer Science+Business Media, LLC
- 出版年份 / Year: 2009
- ISBN: 978-0-387-89193-4
- e-ISBN: 978-0-387-89194-1
- DOI: 10.1007/978-0-387-89194-1

**历史地位 / Historical Significance**:

- ✅ 第一本专门讨论数学中概念映射的综合书籍
- ✅ The first comprehensive book dedicated to concept mapping in mathematics
- ✅ 填补了数学教育中概念映射研究的空白
- ✅ Fills the gap in concept mapping research in mathematics education

### 1.2 核心目标 / Core Objectives

**主要目标 / Main Goals**:

1. **理论理解 / Theoretical Understanding**
   - 理解概念映射在数学教育中的理论基础
   - Understanding the theoretical foundations of concept mapping in mathematics education

2. **实践应用 / Practical Application**
   - 展示概念映射在不同教育层次的应用
   - Demonstrating applications of concept mapping at different educational levels

3. **评估工具 / Assessment Tools**
   - 探索概念映射作为评估工具的价值
   - Exploring the value of concept mapping as an assessment tool

4. **未来方向 / Future Directions**
   - 提出未来研究的方向和建议
   - Proposing directions and suggestions for future research

### 1.3 理论基础 / Theoretical Foundations

**核心理论 / Core Theories**:

1. **Ausubel的有意义学习理论 / Ausubel's Meaningful Learning Theory**
   - 认知同化理论
   - Cognitive assimilation theory

2. **建构主义心理学 / Constructivist Psychology**
   - 知识建构过程
   - Knowledge construction process

3. **Vygotsky社会文化理论 / Vygotsky's Sociocultural Theory**
   - 社会互动与学习
   - Social interaction and learning

4. **元认知理论 / Metacognitive Theory**
   - 学习策略与自我监控
   - Learning strategies and self-monitoring
