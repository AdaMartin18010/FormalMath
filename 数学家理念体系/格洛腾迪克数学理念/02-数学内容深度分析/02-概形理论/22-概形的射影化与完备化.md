   $$D_+(f) = \{\mathfrak{p} \in \text{Proj}(R) : f \notin \mathfrak{p}\}, \quad f \in R_+ \text{ 齐次}$$

3. **射影化结构层**：
   $$O_{\text{Proj}(R)}(D_+(f)) = R_{(f)} = \\\left\{\frac{g}{f^n} : g \in R_{nd}, n \\\geq 0\right\}$$

   $$\hat{X} = \text{Spf}(\varprojlim_n O_X / I^n), \quad I \text{ 理想层}$$

   $$\hat{O}_{X,x} = \varprojlim_n O_{X,x} / \mathfrak{m}_x^n$$

   $$\text{Proj}(R) \text{ 对应分次环 } R = \bigoplus_{n \\\geq 0} R_n$$

   $$\dim \hat{O}_{X,x} = \dim O_{X,x}$$

   $$O_{\text{Proj}(R)}(n) = \text{粘合}(R(n)_{(f)}), \quad R(n)_d = R_{n+d}$$

   $$O_{X,x} \to \hat{O}_{X,x} \text{ 平坦}$$
