## 二、招募通知（院校版）/ Recruitment Notice (University Channel)

---

### 中文版

**致各合作院校数学系 / 理工科院系：**

FormalMath 项目团队现启动课堂验证第二阶段，诚邀贵校本科生参与。详情如下：

**一、研究简介**

FormalMath 是一个面向本科数学教育的开源项目，致力于将传统数学教材与形式化证明工具（Lean4）相结合，打造"银层"级别的双语教学文档。本次课堂验证旨在科学评估新文档相对于传统教材（Strang《Introduction to Linear Algebra》、Mattuck/Lebl《Introduction to Analysis》）的学习效果差异。

**二、招募对象**

- 本科一、二年级理工科在读学生
- 对线性代数或实分析有学习兴趣
- **关键排除条件**：此前未系统学习过对应课程内容（包括自学、网课、竞赛培训等）

**三、验证安排**

| 阶段 | 时间 | 内容 |
|------|------|------|
| 课前 | Week 1 | 基线测试、背景问卷、知情同意书签署 |
| 学习期 | Week 2–11 | 按章节学习、每周学习日志、章节后测 |
| 期末 | Week 12 | 期末综合测试（120分钟） |
| 反馈 | Week 13 | 学生满意度问卷、焦点小组访谈（自愿） |

**四、学生权益**

1. **完全自愿**：学生可随时口头或书面通知退出，不影响课程成绩和学业记录
2. **匿名保护**：所有测试和问卷数据以随机编号存储，报告中不出现任何个人信息
3. **学习支持**：实验组学生可获得项目团队在线答疑支持
4. **参与激励**：完成全部环节的学生将获得项目参与证书

**五、院系配合事项**

1. 协助发布招募信息（海报张贴、班级群通知）
2. 提供一间教室用于期末统一测试（或可安排在线监考）
3. 指派一位协调教师，负责收发纸质材料（如采用纸质测试）

**六、联系方式**

- 项目联系人：FormalMath 验证团队
- 邮箱：formalmath-validation@example.org
- 咨询时间：工作日 9:00–18:00

### English Version

**To Partner Universities — Mathematics / STEM Departments:**

The FormalMath project team is launching Phase II of our classroom validation study and invites your undergraduate students to participate.

**1. Study Overview**

FormalMath is an open-source undergraduate mathematics education project that integrates traditional textbooks with formal proof assistants (Lean4) to create bilingual "silver-layer" instructional documents. This validation study aims to scientifically assess learning-effectiveness differences between our documents and traditional textbooks (Strang, *Introduction to Linear Algebra*; Mattuck/Lebl, *Introduction to Analysis*).

**2. Eligibility**

- First/second-year STEM undergraduates
- Interest in linear algebra or real analysis
- **Key exclusion**: No prior systematic study of the chosen course (including self-study, MOOCs, competition training)

**3. Schedule**

| Phase | Time | Content |
|-------|------|---------|
| Pre-study | Week 1 | Baseline test, background survey, informed consent |
| Learning | Week 2–11 | Chapter-by-chapter study, weekly logs, chapter quizzes |
| Final | Week 12 | Comprehensive final test (120 min) |
| Feedback | Week 13 | Satisfaction survey, focus-group interview (voluntary) |

**4. Student Rights**

1. **Fully voluntary**: Students may withdraw anytime without academic penalty.
2. **Anonymity**: All data stored under random IDs; no personal info in reports.
3. **Support**: Experimental-group students receive online Q&A support.
4. **Recognition**: Completion certificates provided.

**5. Department Support Needed**

1. Help distribute recruitment materials
2. Provide a classroom for the final test (or arrange online proctoring)
3. Designate a coordinating faculty member

**6. Contact**

- Email: formalmath-validation@example.org
- Office hours: weekdays 9:00–18:00
